A framework for understanding how your role must evolve as your business grows — and what it takes to build an organisation that scales beyond you.

Founder Dependency Chasm™

The organisation looks mature. But critical decisions still flow through the founder.

  • This is the most dangerous and most common stage.
  • Businesses get stuck here.
  • Revenue may grow. Organisational maturity does not.

Legacy & Enterprise Scale

The organisation can operate without the founder.
